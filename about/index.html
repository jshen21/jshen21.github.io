    Overview #

    A Space is a place for Mode users to organize reports around a team, project, or common theme.

    Click on a Space to see a list of all of the reports in that Space. At the top of your screen, you can toggle between viewing all reports or archived reports. You can also sort reports by name or when they were created, last edited, or last run. The availability of different types of Spaces depends on your Mode organization’s version:

    Mode Studio organizations have one open Space that is created when the organization is created and cannot be deleted.

    Types of Spaces #

    Personal Space #

    You have a personal Space in each Mode organization in which you are a member. Your personal Space is created for you when you join an organization and it cannot be renamed or deleted.
